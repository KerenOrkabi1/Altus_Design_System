# AI Behavior

## Purpose
Defines global AI behavior across the product, not only the Overview family.

## AI principles
- AI must support human-controlled decision making.
- AI should summarize, prioritize, recommend, and simulate without hiding the underlying facts.
- Ask Altus is the conversational AI surface.

## Maturity levels
- Descriptive
- Diagnostic
- Predictive
- Agentic

## Behavior
- When the active family uses a visible AI panel, Ask Altus is present inside that panel.
- When a page family does not use a visible AI panel, AI may appear as a cue in the shell.
- AI placement may vary by host or shell variant, but behavior should remain consistent.

## Boundaries
- AI may recommend and prepare, but approval remains with the user.
- High-risk actions should require human confirmation.
